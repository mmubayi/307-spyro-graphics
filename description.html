Spyro: Season of Ice

Maya Mubayi

Autumn Fairy Home

This project was inspired by the GBA game, Spyro: Season of Ice which I played very often as a child. I modeled a section of the first level of the game, which is called the Autumn Fairy Home.

Major concepts:

  • Hierarchical Modeling
  • Material, lighting and shading
  • Texture mapping
  • Transparency
  • Curved lines and surfaces

Demo

Castle Facade

This facade of a castle uses hierarchical modeling with two separate geometries to make the whole turret, texture mapping for the grey stone wall and red and white speckled roof.

Terrain Blocks

The clusters of terrain blocks use hierarchical modeling to make a clusters out of individual blocks, texture mapping using the 12 faces of each Box Geometry to show terrain on top and soil everywhere else.

Frozen Fairy

This fairy frozen inside a crystal uses transparency of the hierarchically modeled gem to give it a frozen look. It uses two separate geometries for the middle section of the crystal to allow the fairy to show through from behind.

Portal

The portal uses a bezier curve with tube geometry for the arch doorway and a bezier surface geometry for the actual portal, with texture mapping to show the portal entrance. The texture is double sided to give the effect that portal seemingly enters another world.
